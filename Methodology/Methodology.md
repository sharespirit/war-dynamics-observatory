# Methodology

The War Dynamics Observatory analyzes conflicts using a set of structured indicators designed to reduce narrative bias.

The goal is to evaluate conflicts using observable dynamics rather than political narratives.

---

## Core Indicators

Each conflict is evaluated on a **1–10 scale** across five dimensions.

### Offensive Pressure
Measures the operational momentum of military forces.

Factors include:
- territorial advances
- operational tempo
- initiative on the battlefield

---

### System Vulnerability
Measures structural weaknesses within the opposing system.

Examples:
- economic fragility
- political fragmentation
- logistical stress

---

### Social Resilience
Measures the ability of society to sustain prolonged conflict.

Factors include:
- political cohesion
- public morale
- social stability

---

### War Sustainability
Measures the long-term ability to maintain military operations.

Factors include:
- industrial capacity
- logistics
- financial sustainability

---

### International Escalation
Measures the risk of conflict expansion.

Factors include:
- involvement of additional states
- proxy escalation
- strategic alliances
