# War Dynamics Observatory

An independent observatory analyzing global conflicts through transparent indicators.

## Mission

The War Dynamics Observatory aims to reduce the influence of propaganda and narrative bias in geopolitical analysis by focusing on observable indicators and repeatable analytical methods.

## Core Indicators

Conflicts are analyzed using five indicators:

- Offensive Pressure
- System Vulnerability
- Social Resilience
- War Sustainability
- International Escalation

Each indicator is evaluated on a **1–10 scale**.
